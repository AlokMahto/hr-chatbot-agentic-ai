7.7 Leave  during  the Probation  Period  
 
The objective of probation period as mentioned above is provide time for the new Joinees  
to settle in the Council  and for the Council  to judge whether the new Joinees  is able to 
appreciate the culture of the Council  and perform satisfactorily. Hence, no leave will be 
granted during probation to new Joinees  unless justified as special case  by Departmental 
Head . The leave granted for number of da ys will extend  the probation period by that many 
days.
