4.2 ARMS LENGTH POLICY  
i. Objective  : 
To prevent  any conflict  of interest  of any employee  who  is working  for CHEMEXCIL  and 
having  own  business  or of any  close  relative  aligned to any  activity  of the  Council . 
ii. Applicability:  
This policy is applicable to all employees of CHEMEXCIL  in Staff, Executive, Managerial 
or Leadership  cadre  
a. Council  will not hire  /empanel/  associate  with,  for any business  activity  any 
vendor  or agency  that  is owned/partnered  by any employee  directly  or indirectly . 
b. Not to hire  /empanel/  associate  with,  for any business  activity  any close  or distant  
family  member/  relative  of any employee  of the Council . 
c. An employee  may  refer  a vendor  or agency  for providing  a particular  service  only  
if the above  conditions are  fulfilled.
